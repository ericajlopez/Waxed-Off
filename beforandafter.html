Before Appointment

Hydrate

Drink plenty of water.

Exfoliate

Exfoliate the night before your waxing appointment to help get a smoother pick up.Exfoliate three times a week, every other day (never two days back-to-back).

Moisturize

Do not use a heavily fragranced moisturizer.You can moisturize every day, but
do not exfoliate daily.

No Coffee

No caffeine on the day of your appointment.

No Alcohol

Do not drink alcohol before your appointment. It is a blood thinner and can cause unnecessary bleeding.

No Numbing Cream

Do not use numbing cream or spray. It will not make a difference, and often times it is applied incorrectly.

Post Wax NO'S
for 24 to 48 Hours

Confining clothing
Exfoliating
Exercise
Intimacy
Tanning
Sauna
Fragrant Moisturizers
Swimming (pools, rivers, lakes, etc.)

DO NOT shave between appointments
